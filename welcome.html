TabClose

Automatic tab management for a cleaner browser

What is TabClose?

TabClose is a simple yet powerful Chrome extension that automatically closes inactive tabs, helping you maintain a clean browser and improve performance. No more drowning in hundreds of tabs!

Features

⏱️

Inactivity Timer

Automatically close tabs after a customizable period of inactivity

📅

Scheduled Cleanup

Set a daily time to close all inactive tabs at once

🛡️

Smart Protection

Never closes pinned tabs, active tabs, or tabs playing audio

🔒

Domain Exclusions

Protect important sites from being automatically closed

How It Works

TabClose tracks when you last interacted with each tab and closes them based on your preferences:

  • Inactivity Timer: Tabs are closed after being inactive for your set duration (default: 6 hours)
  • Scheduled Cleanup: Optional daily cleanup at a specific time (e.g., 9 PM)
  • Excluded Domains: Add domains like gmail.com to keep those tabs safe
  • Smart Detection: Automatically protects tabs with audio and pinned tabs

Getting Started

1. Configure Your Settings

Click the TabClose icon in your browser toolbar to open the settings popup.

2. Set Inactivity Time

Choose how long tabs should be inactive before closing (in minutes). Default is 360 minutes (6 hours).

3. Add Exclusions (Optional)

Add domains you want to protect, such as:

  • gmail.com - Keep your email always open
  • calendar.google.com - Protect your calendar
  • spotify.com - Keep music streaming tabs

4. Enable Scheduled Cleanup (Optional)

Set a daily time (e.g., 9:00 PM) to automatically close all inactive tabs at once.

Why Use TabClose?

  • Better Performance: Fewer tabs mean less memory usage and faster browsing
  • Stay Organized: Keep only the tabs you're actually using
  • Reduce Clutter: No more tab hoarding or "tab bankruptcy"
  • Privacy: Your data never leaves your browser - everything runs locally
